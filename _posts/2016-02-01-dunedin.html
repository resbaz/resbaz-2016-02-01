--- layout: site permalink: dunedin location: DUNEDIN dates: February 2-4, 2016 splashImage: https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c9/University_of_Otago_Clocktower.jpg map: sites/dunedin.geojson menu: The Research Bazaar is a (free-of-charge) 3-day intensive conference where researchers come together to up-skill in ‘next generation digital research tools and skills’. In the spirit of a marketplace or bazaar, ResBaz is a highly participatory event where researchers from many different disciplines can learn, share knowledge and skills, and have fun!

Who should attend? Researchers at ALL levels from ALL disciplines. PhD and research Masters students are especially encouraged to apply. Research technologists who support researchers to do their job should also come along!

NOTE! The New Zealand events (Auckland and Dunedin) are being held 2-4 Feb, not 1-3 Feb as elsewhere in the world.

We are still working on the programme to bring you a broad range of digital research skills topics but you should register now to avoid missing out. Below are just some of the sessions that will be on offer.

This focused workshop will help turn your commands into scripts, your scripts into functions, and your functions into packages - what more could you want? schedule: Each day at ResBaz will be split into morning and afternoon events. Following a Key Story from one of our exciting speakers, the mornings will consist of introductory Software Carpentry lessons, alongside intermediate and advanced topics for those who are already familar with the introductory material. Afternoon sessions will begin with a second Key Story, followed by a more informal collection of activities, including focussed workshops, dicussion sessions, and lightning research presentations. At the end of each afternoon we will reconvence for a discusion session, to help set the scene for an evening of fun social and networking activities.
