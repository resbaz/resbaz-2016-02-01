Following a Key Story from one of our exciting speakers, the mornings will consist of introductory Software Carpentry lessons, alongside intermediate and advanced topics for those who are already familar with the introductory material. Afternoon sessions will begin with a second Key Story, followed by a more informal collection of activities, including focussed workshops, dicussion sessions, and lightning research presentations. At the end of each afternoon we will reconvence for a discussion/demonstration session, to help set the scene for an evening of fun social and networking activities.
